**35. What is referral income?**  
Referral income is earned when someone joins using your referral ID and activates a package.

**36. What is binary income?**  
Binary income is earned when both your left team and right team generate business, and the system matches the volume from both sides to calculate your earnings.

**37. How much commission from referrals?**  
Referral commission varies based on the package activated by your referral: Solar Starter 8%, Power Growth 9%, Elite Energy 10%.

**38. What is the binary income percentage for each package?**

**39. What is the binary capping limit for each package?**

**40. How does the binary system work?**  
The binary system matches business volume between your left and right teams to generate binary income.

**41. Is there a capping limit?**  
Yes, a capping limit is applied only on binary earnings, as defined by the platform's daily limits.

**42. How do I get my referral ID and share it?**
